My Favourite Meal - YAM AND EGG SAUCE

My favourite meal is boiled yam and egg sauce. I love this meal beacause it is the tastiest meal I have eaten, it is a complete, balanced diet that gives me all the nutrients I need for everyday, and it is easy to make too

About

YAM:

Yam is a tuber, commonly eaten for its starchy component. It belongs to the carbohydrate class of food. In this article, I will be describing how to make boiled Yam, this meal can be made and enjoyed with any method of cooking yam.

EGG:

My meal is made with poultry egg, which is also eaten as part of a meal. Eggs are full of protein, carbohydrate, fat, vitamins and minerals.

VEGETABLES:

Vegetables are healthy and tasty plants that take meals to the next level. Vegetables that are great for a tasty plate of egg sauce include carrots, spring onions, bell peppers, tomatoes and scotch bonnet to mention a few.

Ingredients

Ingredient Measurement
Tuber of Yam 1
Eggs 4-6
Chopped medium-sized tomatoes 2
Chopped scotch bonnet 1-2
Chopped onion 1
Red bell pepper 1
Vegetable Oil 1/3 Cup
Salt 1/2 tsp (Egg Sauce)
1 tsp (Yam)
Seasoning Cube 1
Curry 1/2 tsp
Thyme 1/2 tsp

BOILED YAM PREPARATION

  1. Slice yam into about 1-inch size and peel the skin
  2. Rinse in clean water till water runs clear and no residue in water
  3. Put yam in a pan, add enough water to cover it, add salt and place on medium heat and cook till soft. Test with a fork, if it goes through, then the yam should be soft enough. Drain water from yam if you want.

EGG SAUCE PREPARATION

  1. Break eggs into a shallow bowl, whisk till egg white and egg yolk are well combined, add salt and whisk to combine then set it aside
  2. Place a frying pan on a medium/low heat, add vegetable oil, heat for about 2-3 minutes
  3. Add chopped peppers and onions (this should sizzle) and cook for about 4-5 minutes or till oil floats on the sauce, add salt, seasoning, curry and thyme.
  4. Stir to combine ingredient with sauce and also avoid sauce sticking to the pan.
  5. Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about a minute, this will allow the egg to set a bit. Stir to scramble egg.
  6. Serve egg sauce with boiled yam immediately.
